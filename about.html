About Me

My name is Benjamin Draves and I'm a Ph.D. candidate in Statistics at Boston University. I work under Dan Sussman studying spectral graph statistics and their properties.

I grew up across the Midwest and graduated high school in Northeast Ohio. I completed my undregraduate studies in 2017 at Lafayette College where I graduated with a degree in Mathematics. My Ph.D. studies began in the fall of 2017 and I now live in Boston, Massachusetts. In my spare time I enjoy playing soccer, running, rooting for the Cavs, and exploring New England.
