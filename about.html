About Me

My name is Benjamin Draves and I am a graduate student in Boston University's Mathematics and Statistics department. I graduated from Lafayette College in 2017 with a B.S. in Mathematics. I have a passion for designing, developing, and applying statistical learning techniques to complex biological systems. Specifically, I am fascinated with the statistical problems that arise in genetic investigations of complex traits and diseases. My current research focuses on estimating genetic relatedness structure within large samples using microarray data. In my spare time, I enjoy watching and playing soccer, indie music, corgis, coffee, and reading.
