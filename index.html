Welcome

My name is Benjamin Draves and I'm a Ph.D. graduate student studying Statistics at Boston University. My research focuses on constructing and analyzing spectral estimators in statistical settings. I work with low-rank estimators in noisy data settings where refinements frequently gleam insight into an underlying probabilistic structure. Oftentimes, these methods prove useful and are applied to data in connectome and genomic sciences.

If you wish to contact me about anything I am currently working on, please feel free to contact me. My information can be found under the About Me tab.
