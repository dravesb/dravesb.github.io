Treelet Smoothers

  Treelet Smoothers is an R package currently in development. Treelet Covariance Smoothers is a class of methods that extend wavelets to non-smooth signals which, through a construction of multi-scale bases, uncover underlying structure in high dimensional, noisy data. These methods are used to improve the estimates of relatedness in GWAS. Established methodology (Treelet Covariance Smoothing - Crossett et al. 2013), as well as new methodologies (Treelet Covariance Blocking and Treelet Covariance Blocked Smoothing - Draves et al. under review) have all been implemented. In addition to these features, cross validation methodologies to choose optimal tuning parameters and visualizations of the hierarchical tree have been implemented.
