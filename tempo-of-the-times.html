Overveiw of Project

This project was completed in the summer of 2015 as a part of the Digital Humanities Scholar Program at Lafayette College. This project looks to connect civil strife with musical trends. A joint project between Vincent DeMarco and myself, Tempo of the Times was hosted at tempoofthetimes.com. We constructed an interactive website to display our results. Unfortunately, due to lack of funding, we were not able to maintain a website of that sophistication.
